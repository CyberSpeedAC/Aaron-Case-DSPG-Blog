Some helpful data found

Farmers Market Prices:
At the 2015 PFI Annual Conference, Kay Jensen collected farmers’ vegetable prices. Recent data shows price ranges for tomatoes, ranging from $2.50 to $4 per pound.

Expatistan:
Tells the price of 1 kg (2 lb.) of tomatoes in Des Moines IA can be used to find other prices located in IA.

AgMRC:
Provides a some detailed and helpful references for tomatoes.

National Retail Report - Specialty Crops ( USDA Fruits & Vegetables Market Report ):

FRED Economic Data:
Contains the average Price of Tomatoes( Tomatoes, Field Grown (Cost per Pound/453.6 Grams) in U.S. City Average) All fresh field grown and vine ripened round red tomatoes. Includes organic and non-organic.
